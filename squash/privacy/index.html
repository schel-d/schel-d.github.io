Squash Scorekeeper Privacy Policy

Squash Scorekeeper does not collect any sensitive user data.

The only persisted data when the app is closed is the current game's status (the score, player nicknames, rules, and serving state), which is stored only on the users device, using Android's Shared Preferences feature.

Google Play or the Android operating system may collect other user data. This is outside the control or scope of the Squash Scorekeeper app.
